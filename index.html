Communication Design / UX Writing

Notification Copy & FAQ Mock-Up

A communication-focused mock-up designed for WhatsApp’s Post-Quantum Cryptography transition. The project focuses on explaining a sensitive security update in a way that feels calm, clear, and trustworthy for a global audience.

Project Overview

This project explored how WhatsApp could communicate a major encryption update without causing confusion or fear among users. Since end-to-end encryption is strongly connected to trust, the communication needed to feel reassuring before anything else.

Instead of focusing on technical explanations, the project focused on user understanding. The notification and FAQ were written using simple language to explain that messages remain private and that no action is required from users.

My role: I developed the notification copy, FAQ structure, and messaging direction while focusing on tone, accessibility, and trust-centered communication.

The Challenge

Encryption updates can easily be misunderstood, especially in regions where surveillance and digital privacy are sensitive issues. Technical wording can increase anxiety instead of building trust.

The challenge was creating communication that explained the update clearly while keeping users calm, informed, and confident that their privacy protections were not changing.

Design Goals

  • Lead with reassurance before explanation.
  • Use plain, globally accessible language.
  • Keep messaging short and mobile-friendly.
  • Reduce misinformation and panic.
  • Clarify that no user action is required.

“Your messages stay private. We’re improving security for the future.”

Notification Copy

The notification was designed as a direct message from WhatsApp. It avoids overwhelming users with technical details and instead focuses on reassurance, clarity, and transparency.

Opening Message

Hi, we’re introducing a new security update to help protect your conversations in the future.

Simple Explanation

This update happens automatically and does not change how WhatsApp works for you.

Privacy Reassurance

Your messages and calls remain protected with end-to-end encryption.

FAQ Mock-Up

The FAQ section was created to answer common user concerns in clear and accessible language. The structure focuses on real questions users may ask after seeing the notification.

Is WhatsApp changing how my messages are protected?

No. Your messages will continue to be protected with end-to-end encryption. This update helps keep that protection strong in the future.

Can WhatsApp read my messages after this update?

No. WhatsApp cannot read or listen to your messages or calls. Only you and the person you are communicating with can access them.

Will this affect my privacy?

No. Your privacy protections remain the same.

Do I need to do anything?

No. The update happens automatically, so you do not need to change your settings or take any action.

Why is WhatsApp updating its encryption?

Technology changes over time. This update helps keep WhatsApp secure against future technological changes.

Design Thinking

The writing style intentionally avoids technical jargon like “Post-Quantum Cryptography” in the first interaction. Instead, the communication focuses on what users actually care about: privacy, safety, and whether anything about their experience is changing.

This approach helps reduce anxiety while still communicating transparency and trust.

Outcome

The final mock-up creates a clearer communication strategy for a sensitive security update. The notification provides immediate reassurance, while the FAQ supports users who want more detail without overwhelming them.

Overall, the project demonstrates how communication design and UX writing can make complex technology feel understandable, trustworthy, and user-centered.
